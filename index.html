Modes of Variability Metrics for CMIP5 Models

This is a PROTOTYPE of interactive parallel coordinate plot that is for Modes of Variability Metrics for CMIP5 models. The Modes of Variability Metrics is developed at Program for Climate Model Diagnosis and Intercomparison (PCMDI) of Lawrence Livermore Natioanal Laboratory (LLNL). Visualization is done by Jiwoo Lee and the metrics were developed by Jiwoo Lee, Ken Sperber, Peter Gleckler, Celine Bonfils, and Karl Taylor. Detailed descriptions can be found at Lee et al. (2018) "Quantifying the agreement between observed and simulated extratropical modes of interannual variability" (Climate Dynamics), especially from Fig. 14. Visualized statistical values are for ratio of simulated to observed temporal variability as estimated by the standard deviations of the PC time series. The CBF PC is used for simulations. For sea-level pressure based modes (PNA, NPO, NAO, NAM, and SAM) in the model results are shown relative to 20CR. For SST based modes (PDO and NPGO), results are shown relative to HadISSTv1.1 The ratios are unitless. Last updated in October 2018.
Usuage:
- Click and drag down on vertical axis to begin brush
- Click vertical axis to clear brush
- Click a label to color data by z-scores
* Please note this page is optimized for screen resolution wider than 1700 pixels.
