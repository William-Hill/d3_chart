Interactive Parallel Coordinate Plot

Statistical values are ratio of simulated to observed temporal variability as estimated by the standard deviations of the PC time series. The CBF PC is used for simulations. For sea-level pressure based modes (PNA, NPO, NAO, NAM, and SAM) in the model results are shown relative to 20CR. For SST based modes (PDO and NPGO), results are shown relative to HadISSTv1.1 The ratios are unitless. Last updated in October 2018, visualized by Jiwoo Lee.
Usuage:
- Click and drag down on vertical axis to begin brush
- Click vertical axis to clear brush
- Click a label to color data by z-scores
* Please note this page is optimized for screen resolution wider than 1700 pixels.
